NPCs

Rathbaran

Otixan

Bronze dragonborn governor of Rathbaran. Desires power and territory. Imagines himself to be just. Dreams of the glory days of the empire. Dragonborn supremacist. Pompous. Values loyalty.

Mishann

Former governor of Rathbaran.

Militia on the Wall

Lilohym

Red dragonborn commander on the wall. Values heroism & skill in battle above all else. Disappointed with the quality of militia, but determined to make it work. Pragmatic. Loyal to her soldiers, Rathbaran, and Otixan, in that order.

Gondring

Aging elf lieutenant. In charge of training recruits. Old guard imperial; served during the golden age and still believes the fall of the empire is a great loss to the world. Stern. A bit brutal in his discipline.

Vrakrinn

Black dragonborn quartermaster. Elitist, dragonborn supremacist. Loves classical imperial music.

Belnys

White dragonborn barbarian, follower of Ceozet. Bloodthirsty. Battle-hardened. Loner. Doesn't respect authority. Mutineer during Beyond the Wall expedition; attacked the party rather than agree to hand over elemental binding stones to the orc shaman.

Brint

Halfling criminal conscript. On galley duty. Coarse. Vulgar. Contemptuous of elites, especially Otixan.

Beluvur

Beluvur is a male tiefling conscripted into the militia. He's a decent scout, but has not found a place on the wall, as he is severely distrusted. Creepy, most find his appearance disgusting. Initially joined Belnys' mutiny, but then thought better of it to save his hide.

Remin

Goliath warrior. Respects and follows strength. Views solutions to problems other than violence with skepticism. Loves overkill. Joined Belnys in her mutiny.

Frollo

Halfling soldier. Not well-trained, but seems to have a knack for fighting and is quite brave. Loves food.

Brooner

Horse.

Suthalion's Band

Suthalion

Half-elf leader of a band of rebels. Believes that all legacy of the empire should be erased; wants to return to the pre-empire ancient past. Collects relics from that past. Doesn't hate dragonborn necessarily, but hates their inherited power and wealth; thinks the rich should be cast down. Cautious. Looking for heroes.

Aladhar

Suthalion's right-hand dwarf. 100% loyal. Hates dragonborn, doesn't care about Rathbaran — would just as soon destroy all former-imperial cities and rebuild, but mostly sticks to Suthalion's party line. Soft spot for defending the helpless. Soft-spoken but firm.

Merjin

Gnome bard, curator of Suthalion's collection and surprisingly powerful spellcaster. Long-winded, very knowledgeable, but actually not that great a performer. Not particularly loyal to Suthalion's political ambitions, but very interested in his archeological research.

Orcs

Old Wise One

Nameless orc shaman, who is seeking to understand the power of the elemental binding circles and what the hobgoblins are doing with them.

Lokhan

Follower of Ceozet (under a different name). Outcast from the white eagle tribe. Covets the headdress.

Ulam

Chief of the white eagle tribe. Untrusting. Proud. Desires the headdress.

Agrigoth

Shaman of the white eagle tribe. Defender or orcish traditions.

Headdress of the Warchief

The headdress is an sentient magic item with Int 14, Wis 10, Cha 19. It can see and hear with darkvision out to 120 ft. It communicates telepathically in Orcish. Its goals are to unite the orcish hordes and dominate all non-orcs. It may compromise the latter objective in the short term to achieve the former in the long term. It hopes for an unending state of war, in which only the strongest survive.

Marska

Thargra's mother. Vicious, competitive, ambitious.

Polg

Thargra's sister. Takes after her mother. Cruel streak.
